About Me

Hello, My name is Braden you've found my website! For much of my life, I assumed that programming was something that only people who loved video games were interested in. When I ended up taking an introductory programming class in college (as a part of a Math degree), I quickly becamed hooked on the joys of coding.

I currently work as a Software Engineer and AI specialist. I love many different forms and facets of programming, from web development to embedded software, but I've recently been enjoying the opportunities to leverage my mathematical and statistical knowledge in conjunction with code through machine learning and AI related projects.

As for the moniker "The Greengo", it's just a simple play on the spanish term "gringo" and my favorite color green. It's been my tag/username on several platforms for years, so if you run across another "the greengo" account out in the internet, it might just be me.
